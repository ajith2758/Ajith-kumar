e-AJITH is an online product selling website development project held under supervision of Mr.Ajith kumar (Faculty at Department of Computer Science, AJITH Bhopal).

This project was assigned as a part of regular project based assignment. The objective of this project is to minimize the difficulties of people by providing them daily needs simply. At e-AJITH people can simply find their desired item and order it. You are provided both choices of "cash on delivery" as well as "Online payment" which you can choose according to your simplicity. We've created an initial prototype of the website.
